Birthright Mars

Securing the Future of Martian Born Americans

Our Mission

At Birthright Mars, our mission is clear: to champion the American pioneering spirit by securing the rights, freedoms, and opportunities of every citizen—whether born on Earth or Mars. We are committed to extending the enduring values and robust legal protections that have made our nation great into the final frontier. As the vanguard of interplanetary exploration, we will harness American innovation and resolve to lead humanity toward a new era of prosperity and discovery.

Priorities

Governance

  • Establish a robust legal framework: Develop comprehensive U.S.-based laws that recognize and protect Martian-born citizens as full Americans.
  • Lead in interplanetary governance: Position the United States as the global standard-bearer in space law and policy, ensuring that American principles guide our expansion into the cosmos.

Sustainability

  • Innovate for self-sufficiency: Drive cutting-edge American technologies that enable sustainable living on Mars and secure a prosperous future.
  • Enhance quality of life: Guarantee that every Martian settlement benefits from advancements in education, healthcare, and fundamental rights—all built on the foundation of American excellence.

Community

  • Forge a united frontier: Cultivate a strong, culturally rich American community on Mars that reflects the pioneering values and shared destiny of our nation.

Path Forward

  • 2025: The Birthright Mars agency is established under U.S. law, marking America’s official launch into interplanetary governance.
  • 2026-2028: Develop and implement comprehensive legal frameworks to define Martian citizenship and secure the rights of American pioneers.
  • 2029-2035: Launch innovative pilot programs in education, healthcare, and infrastructure that lay the groundwork for sustainable, American-led settlements on Mars.
  • 2036: The first generation of Martian-born Americans is formally recognized as full citizens of the United States—affirming our commitment to extending American values beyond Earth.
  • 2037 and Beyond: Continuous American innovation and strategic leadership drive the expansion of Martian settlements, ensuring a dynamic, prosperous future for our nation.
