Birthright Mars

Securing the Future of Martian Born Americans

Mission Statement

To secure the rights, freedoms, and opportunities of every American born on Mars, ensuring they are recognized and protected under the laws and values of the United States. As pioneers in interplanetary life, we commit to leading humanity's expansion into space, promoting innovation, and maintaining America's leadership and prosperity in the final frontier.

Goals

  • Establish a legal framework to recognize and protect Martian-born citizens.
  • Ensure access to education, healthcare, and basic rights for Martian residents.
  • Promote sustainable living technologies to support life on Mars.
  • Position the United States as a leader in interplanetary governance and innovation.
  • Foster cultural identity and community among Martian settlers.

Roadmap

  • 2025: Birthright of Mars agency established by U.S. law.
  • 2026-2028: Development of legal frameworks for Martian citizenship and rights.
  • 2029-2035: Launch of pilot programs for Martian education, healthcare, and infrastructure support.
  • 2036: First generation of Martian-born Americans receives full recognition and rights under U.S. law.
  • 2037 and Beyond: Continuous innovation and collaboration to expand Martian settlements and ensure prosperity.
