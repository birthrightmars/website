About Birthright Mars

Welcome to Birthright Mars – America’s Vanguard in the Final Frontier. Born in 2025 out of a deep commitment to American ingenuity and a proud legacy of exploration, Birthright Mars stands as a clarion call to secure our nation’s destiny in space. Our organization was established by visionary policymakers, industry leaders, and patriots who believe that America’s pioneering spirit is not confined to Earth—it is destined to shine on Mars.

Rooted in the bold tradition of American discovery and self-reliance, we emerged as the definitive advocate for extending U.S. rights and values into the cosmos, inspired by the very drive that launched our first astronauts into orbit.

From our inception, our journey has been defined by rigorous debates over interplanetary citizenship and an unwavering commitment to shaping space policy. We hold a steadfast belief that America must lead on every frontier, including Mars.

Our mission transcends the mere pursuit of space exploration—it is a bold declaration of America’s resolve to innovate, lead, and preserve our national heritage. We are dedicated to establishing a federally recognized Mars agency that empowers the United States to take decisive action in shaping space policy and interplanetary governance.

We invite all Americans who share our vision to join us in this historic endeavor. Together, we will ensure that our nation’s legacy of freedom, innovation, and bold exploration continues to light the way—on Earth, on Mars, and beyond.
